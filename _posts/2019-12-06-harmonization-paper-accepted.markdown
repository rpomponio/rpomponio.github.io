[Permanent link to paper](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuroimage.2019.116450)

This paper facilitated a pooled analysis of 10,477 participants across 18 neuroimaging studies. Using Generalized Additive Models (GAMs), I implemented and validated a statistical framework that flexibly captured differences in brain volumes due to biological variation (age, sex, head size) and removed residual variation attributed to technical noise. I demonstrated the procedure’s ability to improve accuracy in an age prediction task by 11.3%. I published the resulting lifespan volumetric trends online with [shiny apps](rpomponio.shinyapps.io/neuro_lifespan/).
