Raymond Pomponio

I am a data analyst at CBICA, a computational imaging lab. In 2018 I began working here after receiving my bachelor's at the University of Pennsylvania.

I primarily work on the statistical analysis of medical images. Recently, we published a paper on the ability to pool images from different MRI scanners and resolve the inter-scanner differences using a popular method called ComBat.
