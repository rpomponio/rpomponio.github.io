I graduated from the University of Pennsylvania (Wharton School) with a BSc in Economics in 2018. From 2018 to 2020, I was a data analyst at [CBICA](https://www.med.upenn.edu/cbica/), a computational imaging laboratory at the University of Pennsylvania School of Medicine.

My background includes analysis of structural MRIs of the brain, and several of my colleagues have published results with my contributions in [*JAMA Neurology*](10.1001/jamaneurol.2021.0178), [*Brain*](https://doi.org/10.1093/brain/awaa025), and [*Alzheimer's & Dementia*](https://alz-journals.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/epdf/10.1002/alz.12178).

I will be a full-time PhD student in the [Department of Biostatistics](https://coloradosph.cuanschutz.edu/education/departments/biostatistics-informatics) at the Colorado School of Public Health in 2021.
